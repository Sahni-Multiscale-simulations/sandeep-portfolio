Welcome

I am a computational materials scientist passionate about understanding how materials deform and fail at the nanoscale. I use multiscale modeling—molecular dynamics, DFT, and ML techniques—to uncover the mechanisms behind plasticity, indentation, and damage in ceramic and composite systems.

Latest Research

Dislocation-Mediated Plasticity in SiC Polytypes

Using molecular dynamics, I study how stacking sequences in SiC influence plasticity and dislocation motion under nanoindentation.

Nanoindentation of TRISO Fuel Coatings

This work combines micromechanical testing and atomistic simulations to assess the role of porosity, interfaces, and pyrocarbon layers in fuel performance.
