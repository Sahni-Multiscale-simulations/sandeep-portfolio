About Me

Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Western University, Canada. I hold a Ph.D. in Materials Science and Engineering from IIT Kanpur, where I investigated sintering and deformation behavior of Fe–Ni alloys using experiments and molecular dynamics simulations. My broader research spans computational materials science, molecular dynamics simulations, powder metallurgy, and mechanical behavior of advanced materials.

Research Interests

Computational Materials Science

Atomic-scale modeling of materials behavior using molecular dynamics and computational tools.

Mechanical Behavior

Nanoindentation, dislocation plasticity, irradiation effects, and deformation in metals and ceramics.

Powder Metallurgy

Sintering behavior, densification, and processing of Fe–Ni and intermetallic alloys.
