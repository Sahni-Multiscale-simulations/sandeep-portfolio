Latest Work

Dislocation-Mediated Plasticity in SiC Polytypes

Using large-scale MD simulations, I explore how different SiC stacking sequences influence slip behavior, dislocation activity, and transformation under indentation stress.

Nanoindentation of TRISO Coating Layers

This project integrates experimental micromechanical testing with simulations to evaluate the mechanical integrity of nuclear fuel coating layers under localized stress.

Research Interests

I investigate material deformation mechanisms from atomic to continuum scale. My focus includes:

  • • Dislocation dynamics and defect-mediated plasticity
  • • Molecular dynamics and DFT simulations
  • • High-performance computing for ceramics and metals
  • • Nanoindentation and interface mechanics
  • • Data-driven modeling and machine learning in materials
